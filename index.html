Featured Case: Noventiq × Dow Jones

NetSuite SuiteScript NetSuite API Dow Jones Screening & Monitoring API Compliance & Risk Management

Context

Noventiq required an integration with Dow Jones to validate customers against risk lists before processing transactions. I designed a custom integration between NetSuite and Dow Jones’ Screening & Monitoring API to retrieve risk data and match it against customer records, ensuring compliance with internal policies and regulatory requirements.

Complexity

  • Connecting NetSuite with Dow Jones (no existing connector).
  • Matching large customer volumes against global risk lists.
  • Auto-flagging or inactivating customers in NetSuite when sanctions or risks were detected.
  • Coexisting with other integrations while maintaining performance and scalability.

Role

Solution Architect responsible for end-to-end design and delivery, collaborating with a compliance expert to translate regulatory requirements into a scalable technical solution.

Outcome

Successfully delivered a fully operational integration following best practices and SDLC. The architecture was certified by Dow Jones after multiple demos, ensuring smooth and compliant data exchange between systems.
