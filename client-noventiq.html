Dow Jones – NetSuite Integration

Customer: Noventiq

← Back to clients

Context

Noventiq required an integration with Dow Jones to validate customers against risk lists before processing transactions. Designed a custom integration between NetSuite and Dow Jones’ Screening & Monitoring API to retrieve risk data and match it against customer records, ensuring compliance with internal policies and regulatory requirements.

Complexity

The integration involved building a robust end-to-end architecture from scratch, including:

  • Connecting NetSuite with Dow Jones Screening & Monitoring API (no prior connector existed).
  • Matching large volumes of customer data against global risk lists.
  • Automatically flagging or inactivating customers in NetSuite if sanctions or risks were detected.
  • Ensuring the solution operated alongside other integrations while maintaining performance and scalability.

Role

Solution Architect responsible for the full design and delivery, collaborating with a compliance expert to translate regulatory requirements into a scalable technical solution.

Outcome

Successfully delivered a fully operational integration following best practices and the software development lifecycle. Architecture was certified by Dow Jones after multiple demos, ensuring smooth and compliant data exchange between systems.

Tech Stack

NetSuite · SuiteScript · NetSuite API · Dow Jones Screening & Monitoring API · Compliance & Risk Management
